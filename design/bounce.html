Bounce - stickers

Challenge: Design stickers for a hostel in Australia within just half an hour.

After researching hostels in Australia, Bounce immediately stood out to me. The vibrant, laid-back atmosphere of the brand resonated with me, and I felt inspired to incorporate their unique vibe into my designs.

Using Bounce’s color palette and logo as my foundation, I created stickers that reflect their carefree energy while adding a playful twist—a pun that ties into Australia’s iconic imagery. These designs aim to connect with their adventurous guests and represent the essence of the Australian hostel experience. Check out their website.
