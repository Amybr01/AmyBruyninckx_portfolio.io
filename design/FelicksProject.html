Bachelorproef: Felicks

Our project is a bachelor thesis focused on raising awareness about responsible pet ownership, specifically highlighting the responsibilities involved in adopting and caring for a dog through an interactive augmented reality (AR) application. The AR app simulates real-life responsibilities by sending regular, personalized notifications tailored to specific dogs, reminding users of essential care tasks such as walks, feeding, and grooming. This immersive approach aims to provide users with a realistic preview of pet ownership.

Additionally, the app incorporates engaging gamification elements like quizzes and informative articles to educate users about dog care, breeds, behaviors, and suitable lifestyle matches. Users can find potential matches with adoptable dogs from shelters based on both personality assessments and compatibility criteria developed within the app. Complementing the AR app, our project includes a dedicated sales website designed to reflect the unique feel and branding of Felix. This website provides clear instructions for downloading the app and showcases a selection of articles created to inform and engage prospective users. These article pages were specifically designed and developed as part of this project. This bachelor thesis is the result of a collaborative effort by four students. While I was specifically responsible for designing and developing the sales website, each team member contributed uniquely to the success of the project. Check out our individual portfolios here: Hannelore Van Buynderen as lead branding, Isabelle De Haes as project manager, Evelien De Rudder as lead developer (app), and myself, Amy Bruyninckx for the design & development of the website & lead research.
