What I am all about

My name is Amy Bruyninckx, and I am a passionate designer and photographer. I have always been fascinated by the power of visuals and how they can convey stories and emotions. This passion led me to pursue a career where I can blend creativity with technical skills to create impactful designs and stunning photographs.

I chose this career because I believe in the magic of capturing moments and creating designs that resonate with people. Every project I work on is a new adventure, a new challenge to express my creativity and skills.

Story time

During a safari in Sri Lanka past summer, I spent the entire day hoping to spot a leopard. As the sun was setting, I started to lose hope... But then suddenly one crossed the path right in front of us. I locked eyes with it for a fleeting second before it vanished back into the jungle. I didn't capture it on camera, but that pure, unexpected moment will always remain unforgettable.

I am still in awe of that one second when everything came together. Sometimes, the unexpected moments are the most magical.
